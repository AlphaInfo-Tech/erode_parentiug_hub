What we do

Support, information, and a place to belong.

EPH started with empty chairs and quiet rooms. Today it's a network of parents helping each other through every stage — from newborn nights to school admissions to teenage years.

TALK

Peer support groups

WhatsApp and Facebook groups where parents ask questions, share experiences, and get answered by real people — day or night.

LEARN

Expert sessions

Visits and talks with local pediatricians, counsellors and educators — bringing trusted, local expertise to every parent.

MEET

Community events

Carnivals, fun fests and celebrations that bring families together offline — because parenting feels lighter in good company.

Our story

The journey so far

EPH didn't begin with a crowd — it began with a few people who kept showing up.

The beginning

A small group with a big idea

EPH started in Erode with a simple goal: help parents support each other, with empty chairs at early events and barely any replies.

Year one

First events, first friendships

Small meetups grew into the first carnivals and talks — and the WhatsApp group started filling up with real conversations.

Year two

Anniversary at Helping Hearts Trust

A celebration marking real growth — more families, more events, and a community that finally felt like one.

Today

10,000+ parents and counting

What started with quiet effort is now a recognised local community — and it's still growing, one family at a time.
